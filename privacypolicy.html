Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Discord bot (“the Bot”) handles user data.

1. Information Collected

The Bot may collect limited information such as:

  • Discord usernames and IDs
  • Messages sent to the Bot (for command processing)
  • Server, channel, and role IDs (for functionality)

2. How Information Is Used

Collected data is used solely to provide Bot features, such as role assignment, command responses, and community management. Data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Data may be stored temporarily in memory or in files/databases if required for Bot functionality. No sensitive personal information is intentionally collected.

4. Data Sharing

The Bot owner does not share user data with third parties. Data is only accessible to the Bot owner and Discord’s systems.

5. User Rights

You may request removal of your data by contacting the Bot owner. You may also stop using the Bot at any time to prevent further data collection.

6. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored data. However, no system is completely secure, and the Bot owner cannot guarantee absolute protection.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes means you accept the new policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns, please contact the Bot owner through Discord.
